PawPals 🐾
A Smart Pet Care & Service Platform
📌 Overview
PawPals is a pet management and service platform designed to help pet owners manage their pets more conveniently in one centralized system. The platform combines pet care management, reminder scheduling, service recommendations, and pet social matching features into a single application.
The project aims to improve the experience of pet owners by providing:
•	Smart pet care reminders 
•	Pet profile management 
•	Nearby pet service recommendations 
•	Pet matching and social interaction features 


🎯 Problem Statement
Pet owners often face several challenges:
•	Forgetting vaccination or grooming schedules 
•	Difficulty finding trusted pet services 
•	Managing pet medical history manually 
•	Lack of a centralized platform for pet-related activities 
Most current solutions are fragmented across:
•	Notes applications 
•	Social media groups 
•	Google searches 
•	Manual tracking 
PawPals solves these issues by creating an all-in-one ecosystem for pet owners.

💡 Solution
PawPals provides:
•	Centralized pet management 
•	Automated reminders and scheduling 
•	Smart service recommendations 
•	Social matching for pets 
•	User-friendly dashboard
